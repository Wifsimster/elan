Élan is a personal, local-first activity tracker built for cycling and strength training. Your data never leaves your phone: no account, no server, no cloud, no ads.

LIVE CYCLING TRACKING
• Stopwatch, distance, instant and max speed
• Elevation gain and GPS route of your ride
• Live heart rate and estimated calories
• Route map (optional self-hosted tiles, otherwise a network-free vector trace)

STRENGTH TRAINING
• Customizable programs and a daily workout
• Sets (reps × weight), total volume lifted
• Rest timer and estimated 1RM
• Progression tracking, exercise by exercise

BLUETOOTH SENSORS
• Heart rate chest strap (standard Heart Rate profile)
• Cycling cadence/speed sensor
• Automatic reconnection to your last sensor

YOUR DATA, YOURS
• Everything is stored in a local database on the device
• Optional backup to YOUR own S3-compatible server (self-hosted) — your credentials are never shared
• Import Strava / GPX / TCX / FIT files from your phone
• Export your data (Markdown / JSON) via the Android share sheet

PRIVACY
Élan contains no trackers, no analytics, and sends no data to the publisher. Network access is strictly optional and configured by you.

Note: calorie estimates use the MET method. They are rough estimates, not a medical measurement.
